# **App Name**: InvoiceSwap

## Core Features:

- Invoice Upload & Tokenization: Allows SMEs to upload invoice details and tokenize them into tradeable assets.
- Investor Marketplace: A platform where investors can view and purchase tokenized invoices.
- Liquidity Dashboard: Displays the status and value of tokenized invoices for SMEs.
- Automated Risk Assessment: AI Tool to evaluate the risk of each invoice based on payer history and industry trends.
- Smart Contract Integration: Uses smart contracts to automate the transfer of funds and ownership of tokenized invoices.
- Secure Payment Processing: Facilitates secure and fast payment processing for invoice transactions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#2E5CB7), inspired by trust and financial stability. This bold primary color works well in a light scheme.
- Background color: Light gray-blue (#E8EEF7), a desaturated hue that visually aligns with the primary color.
- Accent color: Lavender (#9370DB) for highlighting interactive elements and important information, maintaining an analogous palette that is vibrant yet cohesive.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a sans-serif with a modern feel, ideal for headlines and titles. Body font: 'Inter', a sans-serif suited for longer texts.
- Crisp, minimalist icons representing invoices, investments, and financial data.
- Clean and intuitive layout with a focus on data visualization for clear financial insights.
- Smooth transitions and subtle animations to enhance user engagement and understanding of data.
